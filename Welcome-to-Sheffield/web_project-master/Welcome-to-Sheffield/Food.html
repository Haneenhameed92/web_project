Places to Eat

the best eateries in sheffield

Twisted Burger Company
Twisted Burger Company is an independent company with a focus on good burgers, even if you're vegetarian or vegan. With collaborations with popular music artists from Sheffield, and two locations to choose from in the city, these burgers are not to be missed.

Picture House Social
From the people behind The Great Gatsby on Divison Street, Picture House Social has everything you could want from a place. It is much more than a place to eat - while its street food is the reason it's recommended, it boasts a mini cinema, ping pong room and cocktail bar.

Marmadukes
An independent cafe in Sheffield city centre, Marmadukes are unique for their 'created simply' motto - locally sourced produce and food made from scratch, this cafe is truly part of Sheffield through and through. Go here for a unique experience at a cosy cafe - and try the coffee.

Forge Bakehouse
Another locally sourced business, Forge Bakehouse has a huge variety of baked goods fit for anyone - their bread is unique and even changes throughout the week. Offering a seasonal menu, there is no other choice for baked goods in Sheffield.

Baan Thai
For people looking outside the box and not wanting the regular old burgers or pizza, Baan Thai may be for you - offering wonderful Thai food on Ecclesall Road and an amazing atmosphere, this shouldn't be missed.

Patisserie Valerie
The perfect ending to a day out or a lunch or dinner, Patisserie Valerie offers some of the best hand-made cakes. Their desserts can be a wonderful treat to yourself or someone else, with a wide variety to choose from.
