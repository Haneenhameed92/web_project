What to do when the sun goes down?
Sheffield is well known for its fantastic nightlife. There are a multitude of clubs and bars for people to choose from, depending on their preferences.
If the club scene isnt for you, Sheffield is also host to two theatres and the O2 Arena. So kick back and watch a play or go to a concert and let your hair down!.

Corp is one of the top student clubs in Sheffield! There are three rooms to choose from each with a different music genre to suit everyones taste.
Leadmill is another popular Sheffield club for students. The club makes sure to play a variety of music genres through the night.
West Street Live is a mashup between a bar and a club. It's the perfect place to meet up with friends at any stage of a nightout.
Bierkeller is the perfect bar to visit and have a drink with friends. It's located at the heart of Sheffields nightlife on West Street and has free entry!
The Crucible theatre is the main theatre in Sheffield and it hosts various plays, ballets and musicals to watch.
The 02 Academy is the place to go if you want to go see a concert! Loads of bands and artists tour here making it the place to be for events.
